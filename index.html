Popeyes

Founded in 1972, the Popeyes brand has more than 50 years of history and culinary tradition. Popeyes owes its begginnings to Al Copeland. With one small resturant and a big idea, Copeland introduced the New Orleans style fried chicken that has now made the brand famous throughout the world. Our culinary heritageis built upon the rich Cajun and creole flavor profiles that are mistakably Lousiana. Popeyes distinguishes itself with a unique New Orleans style menu featuring spicy chicken, chicken tenders, fried shrimp, and other regional items.
